About One Piece

One Piece is an anime and manga series by Eiichiro Oda that follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and the Straw Hat Pirates as they search for the legendary treasure, 'One Piece.' They sail from island to island, facing powerful enemies and chasing their dream of becoming the Pirate King.
